How the monthly report works

  1. You upload 4 or 5 weekly report workbooks (output of the Weekly report page).
  2. Each workbook is parsed for its employees, adjustments, category totals and date range.
  3. Optionally paste non-hourly employee names (one per line) — these are subtracted on the Summary sheet. Agency (A-…) names cannot be non-hourly and will be rejected.
  4. Click Process files to validate and store the data in your session.
  5. Click Download Excel to get a combined workbook: one sheet per week plus a Summary sheet with merged employees, category totals, non-hourly adjustments and grouped/non-agency totals.

Weekly workbook (per week)

What it is — the xls/xlsx produced by the Weekly report page (Download Excel on that screen). One file per week.

Layout expected — row 1 contains the Start Date / End Date (D-prefixed); row 3 has the header (Name, Category, SageNo, BasicHours, MonFriOvertime, SatSunOvertime, AnnualHoliday, TotalPaidHours); then employee rows; then an Adjustments block; then Category totals.

Order matters — upload Week 1 → Week 2 → Week 3 → Week 4 (→ Week 5). Week 5 is optional; tick the box to require it.

Non-hourly employees

What it is — salaried / non-hourly staff whose hours should be subtracted from the category totals on the Summary sheet.

Format — one full name per line (case-insensitive). Empty lines are ignored.

Rule — names in an agency category (starts with A-) cannot be non-hourly; the page will reject these.
